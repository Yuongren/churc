About Us

A ministry of Jesus Celebration Center

We are committed to creating an environment where people can encounter God's presence, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose.

Our mission is to be a beacon of hope in the city, bringing transformation through worship, biblical teaching, and genuine fellowship.
